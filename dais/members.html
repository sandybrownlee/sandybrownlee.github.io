Expertise and Applications

Our group's expertise can be summarised by these keywords:

  • machine learning / neural networks / deep learning
  • computer vision
  • optimisation
  • evolutionary algorithms / metaheuristics
  • signal processing
  • explainable and trustworthy AI
  • statistical analysis
  • dimensionality reduction

Our work is a mixture of theoretical development and applied research; applications include:

  • healthcare
  • sensory systems & interpreting sound
  • assistive technology
  • engineering
  • transportation

Some of our group members are also part of the cross-institutional Computational Vision and Image Processing (SVIP) group. You can learn more about this here.
